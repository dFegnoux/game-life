The game of life

If you want to find some information about this game, you may want to read this wikipedia article about game of life.

Controls :
You can click on a cell to input life in it or to kill her
You can also draw on the grid by click and drag
You can drag some famous pattern as glider, zeplins on the grid
When you're done, launch the life simulation simply by press the start button
You can change the simulation speed at any moment by using the speed slider
If the slowest speed isn't sufficient, you can go to the next step by pressing the step button
When you stop life simulation, you will see a heat map of cells lifes, darker colour mean that their were a lot of deaths >:3
